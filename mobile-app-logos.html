Project Overview

This project explores the systematic approach to mobile app logo design through hand-drawn concepts. Each grid represents a different app category, from fitness challenges to photo editing tools, demonstrating versatility in typography, iconography, and brand identity development.

The collection showcases over 200 unique logo concepts, each carefully crafted to communicate specific brand values and user experiences. From bold geometric forms to organic shapes, the designs explore the full spectrum of visual communication in mobile app branding.

Design Process

  1. Research & Analysis
    Studied successful mobile app logos and identified key design patterns and user expectations.
  2. Concept Development
    Created hand-drawn sketches exploring different themes, typography styles, and visual approaches.
  3. Systematic Exploration
    Organized concepts into themed grids, allowing for systematic comparison and refinement.
  4. Color & Form Studies
    Developed both color and monochrome variations to test scalability and versatility.
  5. Final Refinement
    Selected the strongest concepts and refined them for digital implementation.
