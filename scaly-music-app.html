User Journey & Personas

TT

Teacher Tam

High School Music Teacher

Core Needs:

  • Assign structured, engaging practice to students across different skill levels
  • Keep students motivated with interactive, modern learning experiences
  • Find effective tools to reinforce scale theory in and outside of class

Frustrations:

  • Not all students have the same learning pace or instrument focus
  • Students memorize scales in order instead of individual scales
  • Students get bored with traditional theory drills
"I want an engaging, visual, and interactive way to learn scales that fits into my busy routine."
AA

Aspiring Alex

College Music Student

Core Needs:

  • Prepare for music theory exams and performance assessments
  • Strengthen improvisation and songwriting skills by mastering scales
  • Wants structured yet flexible learning that fits into practice sessions

Frustrations:

  • Difficulty in memorizing scale patterns across different keys and instruments
  • Needs instant feedback and interactive learning rather than passive reading
  • Finds traditional theory books dry and hard to engage with
"I want an engaging, visual, and interactive way to learn scales that fits into my busy routine."

Project Overview

Scaly addresses the gap between traditional music education and modern learning preferences. The app combines gamification, visual learning, and structured practice to make scale mastery accessible and engaging for both students and educators.

Design Process

  • User research and persona development
  • Information architecture and user flow mapping
  • UI component design and design system creation
  • Interactive prototyping and user testing
  • Visual design refinement and accessibility considerations

Key Features

The app includes interactive note selection grids, progress tracking with visual feedback, customizable practice flows, and a comprehensive library of premade exercises. The interface adapts to different skill levels and learning preferences.

Technical Approach

Built with modern design principles including clean typography, intuitive navigation, and responsive layouts. The design system ensures consistency across all screens while maintaining the flexibility needed for diverse musical content.
